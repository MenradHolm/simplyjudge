# Shutter Society Judge Quick Guide

This guide is for Shutter Society reviewers using SimplyJudge to score photos and provide member feedback.

## 1. Log In

1. Open the SimplyJudge link provided by the organiser.
2. Log in with your assigned judge account.
3. After logging in, Shutter Society should appear under your events.

If you do not see Shutter Society, contact the organiser so they can confirm that your account has been assigned to the event.

## 2. Start Reviewing

1. Select **Review Photos** for Shutter Society.
2. SimplyJudge will show one photo at a time.
3. Review the image and any available context on the page.

For Shutter Society, the goal is to score every submitted image and leave useful feedback for the photographer.

## 3. Score the Photo

Each scoring category appears in the **Score and feedback** panel.

For each category:

- Enter the score requested by the rubric.
- Use the full range where appropriate.
- Judge the image in front of you, not the photographer.
- Stay consistent with the same standard throughout the review.

## 4. Write Feedback

Use the **Member feedback** field to write comments that are clear, respectful, and useful.

Good feedback usually includes:

- What works well in the image.
- What could be improved.
- One or two practical suggestions.

Avoid comments that mention the photographer's identity, personal assumptions, or anything unrelated to the photo.

## 5. Submit and Continue

After scoring and writing feedback:

1. Select **Submit Evaluation**.
2. SimplyJudge will save your score and move you to the next available photo.
3. Continue until the system says you have scored every photo.

## 6. Change a Score Later

You can go back and adjust scores after seeing more images.

1. Select **My Submitted Scores**.
2. Find the image you want to revisit.
3. Select **Edit Score**.
4. Update the score or feedback.
5. Select **Update Evaluation**.

This is useful if you discover later that an earlier score was too high or too low compared with the rest of the set.

## 7. Finish and Sign Out

When you are done:

1. Open **My Submitted Scores** to confirm your reviewed images.
2. Use **Log out** in the top navigation when finished.

Your submitted scores and feedback are saved automatically when you submit or update each evaluation.

## Quick Checklist

- Log in with the assigned judge account.
- Open Shutter Society.
- Select **Review Photos**.
- Score each image using the rubric.
- Leave constructive member feedback.
- Use **My Submitted Scores** if you need to revise anything.
- Log out when finished.
