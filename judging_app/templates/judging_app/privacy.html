Privacy Policy

Protecting your personal data is highly important to us. We process your data exclusively on the basis of legal regulations (GDPR, TKG 2003).

1. Data we collect

When you register an account or submit a photo to a competition, we collect your name, email address, and the uploaded image file. This data is required to attribute ownership to your submissions and to allow competition organizers to manage their events.

2. Data Storage & Hosting

Our application and database are hosted securely using Render cloud services. Uploaded media is stored securely and is only accessible to authorized judges and competition administrators.

3. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, delete, restrict, and port your data. If you believe the processing of your data violates data protection laws, please contact us at menradholm2@gmail.com so we can resolve the issue immediately.

4. Cookies

This site only uses strictly necessary session cookies required for logging into your account and maintaining your active session. We do not use third-party tracking cookies for marketing.
